About me

I've always harbored a keen interest in a multitude of things, particularly those yet untried. My fascination lies in moments of innovation, where the ordinary reveals extraordinary sparks. This, I believe, is what drew me to HCI - a field where fresh innovations and exciting discoveries merge.

I'm captivated by the underlying causes and reasons behind phenomena - the motivations and behaviors of people, their interrelationships, and the timeless laws that govern the dynamic world. In everything, from science to art and philosophy, I see a common rhythm, a shared breath of life. Uncovering and elevating these patterns brings me immense satisfaction. I am fascinated by contemplating human experiences: how people interact with spaces, their encounters with new products, and their cognitive responses and reactions within novel workflows.

I cherish the process of experiencing and learning. The way my body moves in harmony with music or breath during classical dance and yoga, the deep connection with nature and endurance challenges during outdoor sports, and the intricate interplay of an orchestra's sections - all these experiences are a testament to the constant change yet enduring constancy of life.
